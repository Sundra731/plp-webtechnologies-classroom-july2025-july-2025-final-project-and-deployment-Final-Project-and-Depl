Our Story

Born from Passion

Sunnies is a proudly Kenyan bakery bringing you the taste of home. From fresh chapatis and mandazis to locally inspired cakes and chai, we celebrate the flavors that bring people together. Located in the heart of Nairobi, we blend tradition with creativity to make every bite memorable.

Traditional Methods

We believe in the old ways - slow fermentation, hand-kneading, and patience. Every loaf tells a story of craftsmanship that has been passed down through generations.

Community Heart

More than just a bakery, we're a gathering place where neighbors become friends over fresh coffee and warm pastries. We're proud to be part of this wonderful community.
